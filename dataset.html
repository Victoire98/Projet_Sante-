Base de données d’interet (National Electronic Injury Surveillance System (NEISS))

La base de données collecte les cinq dernieres années du système de surveillance, (2013-2017) from the National Electronic Injury Surveillance System, which is a sample of all accidents reported to emergency rooms in the US. !(code)[https://github.com/hadley/neiss] It currently contains three datasets:

injuries: individual injury results
products: product code lookup table
population: population of the US by age, sex, and year
